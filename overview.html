The OpenCDX Management System is a web-based application designed to streamline the disaster recovery and management process for corporations and their employees.

Key Features

  • Centralized Recovery Platform: Manage disaster recovery procedures from a single platform with real-time availability and updates.
  • Policy Compliance: Enforce disaster recovery policies with built-in approval workflows and budget controls.
  • Detailed Reporting: Gain insights into disaster recovery expenses and procedures with comprehensive reports.
  • Mobile App: Manage disaster recovery procedures and expenses on the go with our fully featured mobile app.

Benefits

  • Reduced Costs: Save money through optimized disaster recovery planning.
  • Increased Efficiency: Simplify the disaster recovery process and reduce administrative overhead.
  • Improved Compliance: Ensure disaster recovery procedures adhere to company policies.
  • Enhanced Visibility: Gain valuable data and insights to manage disaster recovery spending effectively.

Target Audience

OpenCDX is ideal for:

  • Disaster Recovery Managers and Procurement Teams
  • Human Resources Departments
  • Finance Teams
  • Emergency Responders
